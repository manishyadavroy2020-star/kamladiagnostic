Our Services

Comprehensive Diagnostic Services

We offer a wide range of blood tests and diagnostic services to help you monitor and maintain your health effectively.

Complete Blood Count

Full CBC analysis including RBC, WBC, platelets, hemoglobin and hematocrit levels for overall health assessment.

Thyroid Profile

Comprehensive thyroid function tests including T3, T4 and TSH to evaluate thyroid gland performance.

Diabetes Tests

Fasting blood sugar, post-meal glucose and HbA1c tests for accurate diabetes screening and monitoring.

Lipid Profile

Complete cholesterol panel including HDL, LDL, triglycerides and total cholesterol for heart health.

Liver Function Test

LFT panel measuring SGOT, SGPT, bilirubin and alkaline phosphatase for liver health assessment.

Kidney Function Test

KFT panel including creatinine, urea, uric acid and BUN to assess kidney health and filtration.

Vitamin Tests

Vitamin D, B12, iron and calcium tests to identify deficiencies and support your nutritional health.

Full Body Checkup

Comprehensive health packages covering 50+ parameters for a complete overview of your health status.
